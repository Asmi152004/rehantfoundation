About Us

Rehmat Foundation

Our Mission

The mission of the Rehmat Foundation is to enhance the quality of life of individuals and communities by focusing on meeting both their basic and higher needs.

Rehmat Foundation

Our Vision

The vision of the Rehmat Foundation is to develop partnerships with dedicated individuals and existing organizations/institutions to achieve exceptional outcomes with the objective of alleviating poverty, enhancing educational opportunities, and improving health care with special emphasis on mental health.

Rehmat Foundation

Our Objectives

Improve medical education and healthcare with an emphasis on mental health. Improve education in general.

Provide food to patients and their attendants in the hospital who do not have the means to procure food themselves. Provide financial support to orphans

Rehmat Foundation

Improving Medical Education

Rehmat Foundation Global Medicine Initiative strives to facilitate medical education, research, student, and scholar exchange between institutions in India and individuals/institutions in USA to improve healthcare in India.

We have over 90 medical experts in various specialties who deliver lectures at several medical institutions in India via zoom.

Rehmat Foundation

Improving Education

Working in remote areas of Bhopal, Agra, Indore, and Etah to identify and enroll children who are not attending school because of poverty
